Our mission is to help those affected by the ongoing war in Ukraine, providing support, aid, and education. Mending Roots offers assistance to families and individuals in need so they can cover immediate expenses (food, shelter, medication, getting themselves to safety), as well as covering the needs as they rebuild their lives going forward. Working in close partnership with individuals on the ground in Ukraine, we fund our efforts through generous donations like yours.
